About Us

Welcome to ScrapKharkana

Welcome to our website, where we champion the cause of recycling waste for a sustainable future. We are dedicated to the responsible management of resources through the process of collecting, sorting, and repurposing materials. Our mission is to reduce environmental impact, conserve valuable resources, and minimize landfill waste.

Our Vision and Mission

At ScrapKharkhana, we focus on recycling materials such as paper, glass, plastic, and metal, contributing to a circular economy that emphasizes reuse. Through our efforts, we strive to play a vital role in promoting environmental conservation. Explore our initiatives, learn about the benefits of recycling, and join us in making a positive impact on the planet. Together, we can build a greener, cleaner future.

Our Moto

Our main moto is to fulfill the targets of the Sustainable Development Goals
SDG14: Life Below Water
SDG11: Sustainable cities and communities
SDG12:Responsible Consumption and production
SDG15:Life on Land
SDG6:Clean water and sanitation
